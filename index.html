About Me

A bit about myself

PhD
Student

I'm from Fuyang, Anhui. In 2020 I came to Shanghai for my undergraduate degree in Computer Science at East China University of Science and Technology, where I stayed on for my Master's and am now a PhD candidate focusing on Computer Vision.

Beyond research I have a deep love for classical Chinese poetry, history, and literature. Since university I've been composing my own verses — polishing each one late into the night, while happily lost in the craft. Every time I revisit old poems I feel a little embarrassed, but I take it as a sign of growth.

"Each time I reread old verses I cannot help but feel a little ashamed — and I suppose that is exactly what progress feels like."

Quiet by nature, few in friends but rich in quality.
